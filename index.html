HISTORY OF CAMERA

Fascinating History Of Cameras Since The First Camera.

While it’s not absolutely necessary to know the history of cameras to take great photos, serious photographers might still want to learn about it.
The First Camera (Obscura) – 4th Century BC.

So, what is Obscura?

The camera obscura was really the very first camera. However, there weren’t any techniques to permanently capture the projected image, with the exception of drawing or painting.
